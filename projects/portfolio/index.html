Why it helps

Speak, don't type

Reflection is the first thing to slip on a busy take. Portfol.io lets you capture a case in a sentence or two of speech, the moment it is fresh.

Anonymised on device

Identifiable detail is stripped on the phone itself before anything is processed, so patient information never leaves the device in the clear.

Mapped to the curriculum

Each entry is drafted in the expected ePortfolio shape and mapped to the relevant Capabilities in Practice or Generic Professional Capabilities.
